SETUP
After Installation of the APK file, you must first create a devices for first use in
order to automate your home.
1.
Launch the application and click start.
2. Click ADD LIGHT or ADD DEVICE you want to control.
3. Write the device name. And you will see the new devices added immediately.
4.   After your setup, you can now use your Home Automation Application, just click on the
switch you created and it will   control your devices. The light indicates yellow as shown
in the picture   when the light is ON,  same  with the Devices it indicates a virtual switch
that the device is ON or OFF.
5.    You cannot  remove devices once  its   setup unless   you reinstall or clear the date of the
application.   However you can rename the devices anytime by tapping on the name of the
devices.
REMOTE
Using the remote in the application is easy. Just click on the REMOTE button
at the bottom and  you will   be  navigated to the list of   available   devices you  can
remote. Choose what device you want to remote and select a brand.
After selecting your remote will be ready to use. Use the button to navigate the command you
want to use. (Note: the remote uses a Bluetooth connection that navigates to the IR sensor of
the Device hardware, so you don’t need to point your phone into a device you want to remote,
you can just easily tap a button and the hardware will auto remote your device.)
